Story

Sally is a mobile friendly dating website where you can find, like, and message your matches.

Users of Sally can:

  • Have a detailed Profile
  • Upload, make Main, and Delete Photos
  • Determine matches based on gender and age preferences
  • Like other users, and have a list of liked users and likers
  • Message Users - Users have an Inbox, Outbox, and Unread Box
